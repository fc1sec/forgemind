# Tender Analysis and Compliance Project

## Objective
Win a major government tender by preparing a comprehensive, compliant proposal that demonstrates our capabilities and addresses all technical and compliance requirements clearly.

## Context
A strategic opportunity: a government agency is soliciting proposals for a complex services contract. We have the capability but must demonstrate it clearly and meet all compliance matrix requirements. Failure to comply with any mandatory requirement will cause rejection.

## Scope
- Analyze all RFP/RFI requirements (technical and compliance)
- Build compliance matrix mapping each requirement to our response
- Develop technical proposal with clear evidence
- Prepare commercial proposal
- Conduct internal quality review before submission

## Out of Scope
- Pricing strategy (handled separately)
- Organizational changes
- New product development (use existing capabilities)

## Constraints
- RFP deadline is firm (no extensions)
- All mandatory requirements must be met (non-compliance = rejection)
- Response must be <200 pages (be concise)
- All claims must be supported by evidence

## Stakeholders
- **Proposal Manager:** Coordinates proposal effort; owns compliance
- **Technical Lead:** Addresses technical requirements
- **Compliance Officer:** Ensures all compliance requirements met
- **Leadership:** Reviews and approves final proposal
- **Legal:** Reviews terms and conditions

## Current State
RFP received. High-level review shows we can meet all requirements, but compliance gaps identified in early reading.

## Desired State
Compliant proposal submitted before deadline with confidence that all mandatory requirements are clearly addressed and supported.

## Success Criteria
- Zero non-compliance with mandatory requirements
- All claims supported by evidence references
- Proposal complete before deadline
- Internal quality review score >90%
- Proposal passes legal review

## Timeline
- **Week 1:** RFP analysis; compliance matrix creation
- **Week 2:** Technical proposal development; evidence gathering
- **Week 3:** Integration and internal review
- **Week 4:** Final revision and submission

## Notes
This is an all-hands effort. Compliance is non-negotiable. We will use ForgeMind to track requirements rigorously and ensure nothing falls through the cracks.
